Species of shrub endemic to the south-west of Western Australia

Lambertia rariflora
Scientific classification
Kingdom: Plantae
Clade: Tracheophytes
Clade: Angiosperms
Clade: Eudicots

Genus: Lambertia
Species:
L. rariflora
Binomial name
Lambertia rariflora
Meisn.[1]

Lambertia rariflora, commonly known as green honeysuckle, is a shrub which is endemic to the south-west of Western Australia.[1]

The species was formally described in 1848 by botanist Carl Meisner.[1]
